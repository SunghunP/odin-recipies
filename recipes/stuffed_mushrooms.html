Pizza Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 12 Medium-Large mushroom caps, stems removed
  • 1/2 cup of low sugar marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded high quality mozarella
  • pepperoni slices
  • Fresh chopped parley or basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Preaheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. On a 13x8 baking sheet space out the mushrooms and lay them upside down
  3. Fill each mushroom cap with a table spoon of marinara and sprinkle it with mozzarella
  4. Top each mushroom with a pepperoni if desired
  5. Bake until cheese is melted or for around 15-20 minutes
  6. sprinkle with the basil or parsley
